Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with simple ingredients that create a rich and creamy sauce. The combination of eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper results in a comforting and flavorful meal thatâ€™s quick to prepare.

Presentation

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta of guanciale(cubed)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook pancetta
  3. Mix eggs and cheese
  4. Combine
  5. Serve
